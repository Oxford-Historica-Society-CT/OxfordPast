Will Make First Nylon Chute Jump

Caption:

Adeline Gray, 24, who rigs parachutes for the Pioneer Parachute Co. at Hartford, Conn., will make the first test jump in a parachute made of nylon. It will be the intial "live" jump, although dummy weights have been used. -- WIREPHOTO
