Woman Will Jump Sunday
First Parachute Jump by Adeline Gray

GREAT BARRINGTON -- Gus Graf, manager of the Great Barrington airport, has arranged for the first parachute jump of the season Sunday afternoon. The jumper will be a girl, Adeline Gray of Oxford, Conn. She will be the first woman to take a parachute jump from the local field. The event will take place in the middle of the afternoon, weather permitting.
