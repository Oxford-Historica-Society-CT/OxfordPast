MISS ADELINE GRAY, 21, touches up her make-up before taking off to test the first nylon parachute. She rigs 'chutes for the Pioneer Parachute Co. in Hartford, Conn., and will jump from 2,000 feet in the first "live" test of the new silkless life saver. Heretofore, dummies have been used.
