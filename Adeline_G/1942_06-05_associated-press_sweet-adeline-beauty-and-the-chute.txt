Beauty and the 'chute met yesterday at Braiunard Field, Hartford, when Adeline Gray, 24, a parachute rigger, and the only girl in Connecticut licensed as a jumper, prepared to make the first test of a nylon 'chute by a human. Dummy tests have already proved satisfactory.
