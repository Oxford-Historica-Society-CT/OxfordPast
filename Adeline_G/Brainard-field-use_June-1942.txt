State of Connecticut
Department of Aeronautics
Brainard Field

Hartford, Conn.  June 2, 1943

TO WHOM IT MAY CONCERN:

	This is to certify that permission is hereby granted to Adeline Gray of Manchester, Connecticut to make a test parachute jump at Hartford, Connecticut, on June 3, 1942, in accordance with the provisions of Special Regulation 42-4.

	The parachute to be used in this instance shall be one manufactured by the Pioneer Parachute Company, Inc. of Manchester, Connecticut, with a main parachute of not less than twenty-four feet in diameter and a reserve parachute of not less than twenty-two feet in diameter.

Kemneth H. Ringrose
Deputy Commissioner
