Derby Colonial Cemetery
Derby, New Haven, Connecticut
Elijah  Humphreys



To
Elijah
son of William and Maria Humphreys
in fond commemoration
of his rare promise
Premature mental endowments
high Christian principles
manly modesty and gentle grace of manners
this stone is inscribed
Born May 19 A. D. 1821
In the flower of youth and early hope
at the age of twelve years
he was not for God took him
Octobert 22  1833
and after a dreary interval revealed
the mistery of his untimely death
Jan 31  1835
His remains recovered from the neighboring waters
Here await the issues of that day
When united with kindred dust
Mortality shall be swallowed up of life
And the Lord shall make up his jewells.

Orcutt, Rev. Samuel,  History of the Old Town of Derby, Connecticut, 1642-1880,
   (Springfield, Massachusetts, Press of Springfield Printing Company, 1880)
page 809
