#16  Hillside cemetery

    The tombstone pictured belonged to Captain Zachariah Hawkins.  The inscription is faded, but historian William C. Sharpe recorded it a century ago.  It reads:   


      "By this stone are deposited the remains of Capt. Zachariah Hawkins, A worthy and respectable member of society. He had 14 children who all survived him, 82 grandchildren and 95 great-grandchildren.  Sarah, his first wife, is buried in Derby.  Mary, his second wife, is buried 12 feet on the left of this tone.  Rachel, his third wife lies close by on the right.  Lydia, his relict and sons erect this monument, their tribute of gratitude, love and honor."
