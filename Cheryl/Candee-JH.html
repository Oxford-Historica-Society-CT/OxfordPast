Oxford Past Family Genealogy Project
CANDEE

Five generations buried at Jacks Hill Cemetery

  
   The Candee family buried at Jacks Hill Cemetery are all descendants of 
Samuel Candee  (1678 - 1749)   m.  28 Apr 1703  Abigail Pineon  (1680 - 1742)
They died at West Haven, New Haven, CT., and are buried there.
    There are 21 known descendants of Samuel and Abigail buried at Jacks Hill.

1st  generation - 1 son
2nd  generation - 3 grandchildren
3rd  generation - 6 great-grandchildren
4th generation - 10 gr-great-grandchildren
5th generation - 1 gr-gr-great-grandchild

First Generation

Samuel Candee   (1705 - 1775)    m.  1 May 1729  Mehitable Smith   (1709 - 1799)
is not buried at Oxford, but one of his children is buried at Jacks Hill Cemetery.

The youngest son of Samuel Candee and Abigail Pineon,
Caleb Candee   (1722 - 1777)    m.   Louis Mallory   (1721 - 1790)
is buried at Jacks Hill Cemetery with his wife and 19 descendants.

   There is an  unusual looking stone found beside the headstone of Caleb and Louis Candee. 
The stone  that says CANDEE, belongs to John Candee,  a gr-grandson of Caleb and Louis.
