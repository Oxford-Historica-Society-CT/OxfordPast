Sarah\Sally CHATFIELD

HUNTINGTON BURYING-PLACES, STRATFORD, CONNECTICUT

Dan Chatfield, Born 1761, died 1845.  A veteran soldier of the war for American Independence.
Sarah Chatfield, daughter of Dan Chatfield, and wife of Lemuel Summers,
    Born Aug. 28, 1788,. Died Jan. 8, 1873.
David Chatfield, Born June 8, 1794, died June 30, 1836.
Nancy, his wife, born April 6, 1796, died Aug. 25, 1879; daughter of Dan Chatfield.
Erected by David H. Chatfield, son of David Chatfield.
John E. Chatfield Died Jan. 5, 1877. Aged 66 Years.
Asleep in Jesus blessed sleep,
From which none ever wake to weep.
