Clara Belle COOPER

Died at Colorado State Mental Health Institute at Pueblo, CO
History at time of commitment:  Subject to fits since about eight years of age.  Quitet in childhood, and continued to do well in her school work until about twelve years of age, when she suffered a very severe attack of typhoid fever, since which time has been prone to epileptic fits.  Is gradually becoming worse, and harder to manage.  At times shows (--), and inclination to do injury to others. (13 June 1918)  Physical examination:
Small, slender built girl; stands erect; not very well nourished; weight about 80 lbs; 4 ft 11 inches;
complexion is dark: eyes, brown; hair, brown; skin, moist. Race:  Mixed (?)  Died of lobaria.
