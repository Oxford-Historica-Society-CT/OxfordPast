Charles H. DAMAN

CHARLES H. DAMAN (1800's-1900's):  Charles moved to Detroit in 1920 and lived at 391 Henry St.  In 1923 the address was 1357 Henry Street.  
     He worked as a carpenter in Detroit in 1923.  
     Leonard Daman (eldest living son of Arthur E. Daman) stayed with Charles a day or two when he moved to MI in 1923.  Leonard says Charles had a dau., Ruth.
     Was in Marshall, IL in 1860 (note: it could have been his father who was in Marshall, IL).
Nurse family records.
Detroit City Directories, 1920-23.
