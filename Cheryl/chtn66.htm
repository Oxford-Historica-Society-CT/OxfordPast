Thomas LATHROP

Thomas Lathrop moved from Cherry Burton to Etton, Hartill Wapentake, East Riding, Yorkshire, England about 1576.  He dated his will Oct 05, 1606; it was proved Jan 1607.
    Thomas Lathrop (b. 1536) by his second wife Mary Howell, is the father of the famous Rev. John Lathrop, minister to the Pilgrims at Plymouth and Barnstable, MA?  Rev. John was bp. 20 Dec 1584 at Etton, Yorkshire, England, and this seems to fit your data.
