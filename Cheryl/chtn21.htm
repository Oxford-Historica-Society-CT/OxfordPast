Hiram S. DAMAN

HIRAM S. DAMAN (1835-1893):  CENSUS 1860 IL:  He was in Marshall, IL in 1860 (according to Marshall, IL CENSUS records for 1860), Hiriam was 24 and Jerusha was 21.  Edwin was one month at that time.  "Nurse Family Records" show Hiram's death as Jan 25, 1893 in Adair, MO.
