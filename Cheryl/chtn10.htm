Gershom LAKE

1790 census Castleton VT: Gershom Lake
Died 7 May 1792, age 60 at Castleton VT (b 1732), native of Woodbury VT, settled in Castleton 1771. Enlisted April 13, 1778 in Col. David Worster's Co. to invade Canada.
Served in Amer. Rev. b near the present (1907) site of Bridgeport VT in Stratford Plantation.
b. prob. of New Milford CT.  (LDS film 928213)
IGI: b 21 Jun 1739 Litchfield CT.
1800 cen. Castleton VT: Sevia Lake, 3F 16-26, 1F over 45.

William Franklin CHATFIELD

WILLIAM FRANKLIN CHATFIELD (1842-0000):  "The Chatfield Family" does not state who Williams father was.  This is an interesting mg.  William resided in Waterbury, New Haven, CT where he was a contractor and builder.
"The Chatfield Family", by William C. Sharpe, 1896, Seymour, New Haven, CT.
Genealogy.com (LLC), Broderbund (FTJ), Vol. 73, Tree 0201.

Enos CHATFIELD

ENOS CHATFIELD (1797-1869):  Enos was a member of the 6th Company of the 2nd Regiment, CT Militia, in Sep 1819.
"Connecticut Genealogies #1, 1600s-1800s, Families of Ancient New Haven", Vol. VII, pg. 1693.
