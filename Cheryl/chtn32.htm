Harvey W. , Twin CHATFIELD

HARVEY W. CHATFIELD (1819-0000):  Harvey was a twin brother with Henry.  He represented the town of Derby, New Haven, CT [at Oxford]  in the state General Assembly of 1878.
    Will of Harvey signed at Oxford, New Haven, CT  names "my wife Harriet Esther Chatfield" and "my son Edward A. Chatfield".

Edward Aurelius CHATFIELD

EDWARD AURELIUS CHATFIELD (1848-1894):  Testator of Apr 20, 1890.  Bequeaths made to "my beloved mother Harriet E. Chatfield"  --- "my beloved wife Eliza (Breck) Chatfield" --- and to "my beloved dau. Alice J. Chatfield".
