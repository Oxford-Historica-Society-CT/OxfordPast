Sally MABEY

Sally Mabey was mentioned, as his daughter, in the will of Solomon Mabey who died Dec 18,1850 Westerlo, Albany County, New York. "Will of Solomon Mabee, of Westerlo, Albany County, dated Jun 12, 1848, proved Jan 28, 1851, mentions sons Solomon, Daniel, Alanson, William N., Gilead, and Abraham; also mentions daughters Sabrina (wife of Oliver Bryant), Jerusha (wife of Harvey Kirkland), Sally (wife of Curtis Lake), Rosanna (wife of Anias Whitford) and Samatha (unmarried)"
