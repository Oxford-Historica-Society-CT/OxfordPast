Chester CHATFIELD

CHESTER CHATFIELD (0000-1890):  Chester was a member of the 6th Co. of the 2nd Reg., CT Militia in Sep 1819.
    Testator of Jun 24, 1875, he bequeaths:  "to my wife, Cynthia Chatfield", . "four sons Philo, Harvey, Henry and Wilbur"  . and "to my grand-dau. Jennette E. Chatfield dau. of my son Eugene deceased, as a kindly remembrance to her  .".  He states he is "of the town and Co. of New Haven and state of CT".
Genealogy.com (LLC), Broderbund (FTJ), Vol. 73, Tree 0201.

Cynthia BUCKINGHAM

CYNTHIA (BUCKINGHAM) (MORRIS) CHATFIELD (1804-0000):  In Cynthia's will she states she is of New Haven, New Haven, CT and bequeaths "one half the net income thereof to my son Wilbur F. Chatfield during his life, the other half to his wife Elizabeth during her life provided she remain his Widow".  Further provisions made for any child or children b. to Wilbur and Elizabeth.

Lewis CHATFIELD

LEWIS CHATFIELD (1787?-1858):  The Lewis Chatfield who d. at Oxford, New Haven, CT  on Mar 30 1858, aged 71, leaving a Widow, Thirza, who d. Sep 13, 1890, aged 92.
Genealogy.com (LLC), Broderbund (FTJ), Vol. 73, Tree 0201.
