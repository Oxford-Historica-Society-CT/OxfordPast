My wife,
Glenda (Lamb) Ball is the oldest grandaughter of
Frank and Nellie Nicholson Morgan.      We are just beginning to try to learn more of his ancestry.
                   My wife's mother,
Vontelle Morgan Lamb is a daughter of the Morgans.  She  will be 94 years old on Aug. 5th.
                   We have  a partial record of Frank's parents,
William and Martha Jones (Darst) Morgan.  William was a son of  
Joseph and Lydia Thomas Morgan.    We know very little about their family.  He was a circuit rider.                  We have a picture of both Joseph and Lydia.  We do not know                 who Joseph's parents were.
                   We would be happy to hear from you.
Harold Ball
