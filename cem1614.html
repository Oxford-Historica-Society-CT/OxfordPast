Hillside (Hawkins) Cemetery

Ann  Moss



Joseph Wooster,
died Dec. 18, 1819,
in his 76th year.

 Samuel Wooster,
his father,
died Sept. 16, 1776,
in his 70th year.

 Ann Wooster,
his mother,
died Sept. 14, 1791,
in her 81st year.


   His children

 Dolly, died Mar. 7, 1777, aged 3.
 David, died Jan. 15, 1778, aged 3 years.
 Lucy, died Nov. 20, 1783, aged 1 year.
                                                                                

(Excerpt from
History Of Oxford,
by W. C. Sharpe,
page 84.)
NOTE: 
  Daughter is recorded as Dolly on this page, but recorded
elsewhere as Polly. The letter "P" is clear on the stone shown below.
