MARY  P  HULL
wife of
Henry S Hull
Died March 9 1863
aged 54 years.

There is a land mine eyes have seen
In visions of enraptured thought
So bright that all which spreads between
Is with its radiant glory fraught
A land upon whose blissful shore
There rests no shadow falls no stain
There those who meet shall part no more
And those long parted meet again.

Orcutt, Rev. Samuel,  History of the Old Town of Derby, Connecticut, 1642-1880,
   (Springfield, Massachusetts, Press of Springfield Printing Company, 1880)
page 819
