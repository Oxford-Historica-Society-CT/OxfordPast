Lucy  Matilda  Bassett


August 2007


Lucy M
daughter of
Sheldon and Harriet Bassett
died Aug 28 1831
aged 15 months and 8 days.

  She's gone but where? Ah pause and see
  Gone to a long eternity
  She's gone but where from toil and pain
  To where eternal pleasures reign
  She's gone before her mind could know
  The ills attending all below.
Orcutt, Rev. Samuel,  History of the Old Town of Derby, Connecticut, 1642-1880,
   (Springfield, Massachusetts, Press of Springfield Printing Company, 1880)
