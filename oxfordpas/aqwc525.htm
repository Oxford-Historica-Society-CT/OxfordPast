1Rev. Hollis A. Campbell, William C. Sharpe and Frank G. Bassett, Seymour Past and Present (Seymour, Connecticut, W. C. Sharpe, 1902), p. 403. "Deacon Nehemiah, son of Nehemiah and Mercy (Johnson) Botsford, married Lucy, dau. of Abel and Ruth (Curtis) Waters, of Oxford, Ct. ... Lucy died Feb. 11, 1856, aged 79." NOTE:
    This would have Lucy born 1776-77. Sharpe's History of Oxford, p 34, gives the marriage of Abel Water and Ruth Curtis as Christmas Day, 1788.  http://www.our-oxford.info/Books/sharpe/034.html   
    The 1934 WPA list for the Seymour Congregational Cemetery reads: Botsford, Lucy, wife of Nehemiah, died Feb. 11, 1857, aged 73 yrs. This would give her birth year 1783-84.
    At this time is seems unlikely that Lucy was a daughter of Abel and Ruth (Curtis) Waters.
