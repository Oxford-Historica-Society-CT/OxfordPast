Many people gathered at the Stevenson Dam, on March 10, 1935, to view the water. More water poured over it then than at any time in its previous history. Torrents of water plunged over the huge spillway, sending up clouds of mist and spray.  Notice the cars and spectators on the bridge. (Photo from Oxford Historical Society Collection.) (From Images of America, Oxford, by Dorothy A. DeBisschop)
