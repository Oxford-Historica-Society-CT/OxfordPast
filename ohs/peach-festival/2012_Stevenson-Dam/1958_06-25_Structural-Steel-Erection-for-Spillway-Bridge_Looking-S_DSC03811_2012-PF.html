Structural Steel for the Spillway bride, June 25, 1958.  Photo shows the change that will be made from the older sharp approach to the bridge. The power station on the Monroe side of the bridge is in the background. To the far right you can see the sheet piling which held the waters back while the Taintor Gates were being constructed. (Photo Courtesy of Blakeslee Arpaia Chapman, Inc., Branford.  Their parent company, C. W. Blakeslee & Sons built the Stevenson Dam in 1917.)
