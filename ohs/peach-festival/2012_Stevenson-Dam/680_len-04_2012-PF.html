Photo of Bridge Toll-keeper's house and store. See news articles of the New Haven Register, Nov. 3, 1891 and Nov. 17, 1891, in which Mr. Stowe, who claimed ownership of the store was ordered to vacate the premises, because the County Commissioners contended it was a hazard to the wooden towers which held the suspension bridge, recently turned over from the Towns of Oxford and Monroe to New Haven and Fairfield Counties.
