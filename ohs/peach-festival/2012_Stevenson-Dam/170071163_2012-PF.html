This scenic view of Lake Zoar after the construction of the dam and the bridge across it, shows a couple by the fence enjoying the view of the serene lake and countryside. The lake is approximately ten miles long, with a shoreline of 27 miles, offering summer and winter recreation, as well as the source of electrical power. (Courtesy of Ann Hunihan Ludgewait.) (From Images of America, Oxford, by Dorothy A. DeBisschop)
