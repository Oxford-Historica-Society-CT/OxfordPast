Dairy farmer Peter Popowski delivered his milk in 40-quart milk cans by horse and wagon to the Stevenson Bridge pickup station. The milk cans from a variety of farmers were picked up by the dairy at this central point. The picture shows Barbara Jean Saad in Popowski's wagon. Popowski in seen behind the horse. (Courtesy Howard Saad.) (From Images of America, Oxford, by Dorothy A. De- Bisschop)
