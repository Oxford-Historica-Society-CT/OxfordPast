Photo of C. W. Blakeslee work in initial construction of the Stevenson Dam.  Both the Oxford and Monroe side of the dam have been constructed, but the center portion is still open.  The structural work in the foreground is on the Monroe side. The two stacks on the right are also seen from another perspective in the photo of the Mixing and Power plants.
