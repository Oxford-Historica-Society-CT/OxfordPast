Frederic French, a resident of Freeman Road, is shown with his prize oxen. When the Stevenson dam was built, Mr. French recycled some of the construction lumber and portions of the old bridge, using this yoke to haul the wood to his home to build a porch.   (Courtesy of Joseph Lenahan.) (From Images of America, Oxford, by Dorothy A. DeBisschop)
