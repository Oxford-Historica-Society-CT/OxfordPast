Eugene Agafonoff, Russian artist, came to Oxford after the Bolshevik revolution and settled in Riverside. His daughter, Eugenia Purcella, was clerk of the Oxford Probate Court and his granddaughter, Susan Purcella, is a teacher at Great Oak Middle School in Oxford. (Courtesy of Eugenia Purcella.)
