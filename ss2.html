#2  Skeel-Davis House    This house on Bowers Hill Road was built in 1710 by Anson Skeel.
    Another Davis built a home off Great Hill on what was to become Oxford’s most famous fruit farm. Today the area is known as Peach Farm Road in honor of its history.  There is also a Davis Road, off Hogback Road, which is named for the descendants of Col. John Davis.
