2019_10-13_101_0120.jpg   Little Red Schoolhouse visit 10/13/2019

Talked with docent who led the classes. They do the second grade. They have wall board desks and attached benches. She goes over the Three R's:

READING: Use McGuffey Readers. Have made individual booklets, stapled copies from the McGuffey Readers. Starts with a few pages from the first reader, then some from the second, third and fourth readers. Shows them how accent marks are used in the text, how the text also includes cursive writing. Each student uses an individual photocopied reader. They get to read aloud together and singly, if time permits.

WRITING: She writes a phrase or sentence on the main schoolboard, and the students all copy that, using quill pens.

ARITHMETIC; She puts four arithmetic equations on the board, and each student solves them, one at a time on their little chalk board with chalk. They solve the first one, and when the teacher or assistant say it is correct, they can go on to the next one, and through the four equations.

SUPPLIES: Little chalk board for each student, chalk, quill pens, ink, McGuffy reproduction compilations, Graces, other period games for outdoors.
