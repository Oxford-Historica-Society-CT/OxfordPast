Preparing and Moving the Munn Schoolhouse
Interior and exterior of the building was fully braced and reinforced to prevent warping or
breaking up during transit with $9,000 grant from the Valley Community Foundation.

 2) Exterior walls were all braced to
reinforce building structure.
 3) Roof was disassembled to allow
safe passage under power lines.
 4) Temporary cover to protect from
rain while waiting for moving day.
 5) Rear needed extra reinforcing
due to weakened corner beam.

MOVING DAY: November 13, 2019
All that preparation produced a problem-free, safe relocation of the building from
561 Oxford Road to Twitchell-Rowland Homestead Museum site at 60 Towner Lane.
Structural Mover cost of $18,500 was paid through a Town of Oxford Community Support Grant.
