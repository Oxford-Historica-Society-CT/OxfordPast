After being at sea some days, they were overtaken by a heavy eastern gale which lasted fourteen days. When the gale had abated they found themselves near the New York harbor. The ship was then in a sinking condition, and in order to save their lives, they ran the ship ashore at a place called Amboy, New Jersey, Dec. 18, 1685.
