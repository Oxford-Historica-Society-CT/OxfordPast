Derby Colonial Cemetery
Derby, New Haven, Connecticut
Doctor  Edward  Crafts










August  2007


Doct Edward Crafts
Dea of the Presbyterian Church
and for many years a successful
Physician and influential citizen
of Derby. He was born at
Pomfret April 19 1752 and died
March 17 1821 aged 69 years.
   
Mrs Melissa
third wife of
Doct E Crafts
died Jan 19 1844 æt 75.


Mrs Abigail
first wife of
Doct E Crafts
died Jun 8 1813
æ 56.
Children
of Doct Edward and
Mrs Abigail Crafts
   
Julia died Sept 16 1801
æ 20.
   
Laura
died June 15 1805
in the 16 year of her age.
   
Edward
died Feb 20 1792
æ 2.
   
Abba
died Aug 24 1811
æ 15.


Mr Samuel Crafts
was lost at sea
in lat 39 N Long 39 W
June 5 1810
æt 27
 
Edward Crafts
died Nov 1826
æt 32
