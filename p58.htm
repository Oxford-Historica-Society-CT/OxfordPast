THE CHURCHES IN OXFORD, 1973

Christ Church, Quaker Farms
St. Peter's Episcopal Church,
Oxford United Church of Christ, Congregational
Immanuel Lutheran Church
Riverside Methodist Church
St. Thomas the Apostle Church


    All the above churches in Oxford have some form of a printed church history, the records of the two Episcopal Churches being the most complete, as they were compiled and published as separate hard-cover books by the late Norman Litchfield.
    The Congregational, Lutheran and Catholic churches all have reviewed their history on the occasion of special events when commemorative booklets have been published by the church.
    A History of the Pleasant Vale Church, or the Riverside Methodist, was written by Miss Johanna Busk in 1968.
    All of the foregoing have been used to present to readers of this history a general view of the development of the various denominations within the community of Oxford, Connecticut.
