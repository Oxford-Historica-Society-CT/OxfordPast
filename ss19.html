#19  Wooster House
    On Route 34, near the Seymour-Oxford town line, there stands this large two-story house. It is thought to have been built around 1731.  An old map of the property shows that it was owned by the Wooster family of Derby in the 1800’s.  Some historians believe that David Wooster, who died a patriot hero at Danbury, lived here as a youth.
