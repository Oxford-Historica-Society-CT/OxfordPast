#14  Jacks Hill Cemetery
        Here at old Jack’s Hill Cemetery, are several interesting gravestones. This is the burial place of Phineas Johnson, an American Revolution veteran.  He was in several battles, and served under General Wayne, and with George Washington at Stony Point on the Hudson River.
