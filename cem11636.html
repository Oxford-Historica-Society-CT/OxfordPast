Derby Colonial Cemetery
Derby, New Haven, Connecticut
Elizabeth  Coe



In memory of
Miss Elizabeth Coe
dau of Mr John and
Mrs Hannah Coe
who died of the Small
Pox May 24
A D 1795 in the 22 year of her age
 
Ye youth and gay
Attend this speaking stone
Think on her fate
And tremble at your own
Orcutt, Rev. Samuel,  History of the Old Town of Derby, Connecticut, 1642-1880,
   (Springfield, Massachusetts, Press of Springfield Printing Company, 1880)
