Welcome
  
  Oxford Past is a joint effort dedicated to providing a free database of Oxford history online.  Our goal is to gather information, to create a resource for the Oxford area, that is as  accurate and informative as possible.

  Special thanks to all those who have provided their efforts to record our history.

   We are working hard to ensure that all work is given proper credit.

  All additions and corrections are appreciated.
