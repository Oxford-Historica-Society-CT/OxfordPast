PREFACE
    The proper study of history is biography - the lives of those who lived in the past show the total history of their times. From this collection of biographies, one develops an historic overview of the development of the Lower Naugatuck Valley. The biographies are a representative sampling of Valley leaders, from 1667 until 1910. Included are persons from the following Connecticut towns:




     Seymour, Oxford, Beacon Falls, Ansonia. Derby and Shelton.
