1884_02-15_Seymour-Record

OXFORD

The Guild will meet this week at the rectory.

Rev. Mr. Bailey is expected back again on Saturday.

The grand list has been reduced about $1,000 this year.

There has been a club raised in the center for five of the most popular magazines.

Miss Lois Lum started on Tuesday for Ripon, Wis. where her father is quite low.
