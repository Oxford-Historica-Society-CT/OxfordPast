1884_01-25_Seymour-Record

OXFORD

C.F. Beardsley of Five Mile Hill is improving.

Sidney Downs has had another severe attack and is not expected to survive many hours.

E. B. Alling, eldest son of E. J. Alling, now living at Branford has purchased of John B. Pope, the Cressy place on Christian Street.

Several persons including the musicians, Messrs. Stuart and Robinson, attended a party at Millville on Wednesday evening.
