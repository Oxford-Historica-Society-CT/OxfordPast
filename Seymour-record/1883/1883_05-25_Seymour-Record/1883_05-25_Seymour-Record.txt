1883_05-25_Seymour-Record

OXFORD.

Judging from the very full blossoms this is a fruit year.

Frank B. Andrews horse got mired in the swamp last Sunday and was only extricated with considerable difficulty.

Mr. and Mrs. O. C. Osborn, Mrs. Nehemiah Andrew, W. W. Hughes and Horace Hinman, attended the formal opening of the Brooklyn Bridge on Thursday.

Rev. Mr. Upson of New Preston supplied in the Congregational church last Sunday. Rev. Mr. Snow is expected to be present next Sunday.

Melville R. Bailey, who is to be ordained at Middletown on the 30th, is to go to Waterbury as assistant to Rev. Mr. Converse of St. John's church, and also as instructor at St. Margaret's school of that city.
