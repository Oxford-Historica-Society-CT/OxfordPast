1883_04-20_Seymour-Record

OXFORD.

To all whom it may concern. Beware of the new post near the post office.

James P. Riggs has vacated F. Lum's and occupies a portion of O.C. Osborn's house, near the Riggs street school house.

H. E. Bidwell returned to Rome, N.Y., last week for further treatment, and it is since reported that he is very low.

Bishop Williams appointment at St. Peter's church next Tuesday, 24th, is at 10:30 a.m., instead of 2 p.m. as previously announced.

Several from this place attended the G.A.R. Fair in Seymour last Wednesday evening.
