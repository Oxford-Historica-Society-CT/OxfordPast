1883_05-11_Seymour-Record

OXFORD.

Rev. John Anketell, formerly rector of St. Peter's parish, made a visit to town last Wednesday.

Rev. W. C. Roberts will preach and celebrate the holy communion in Christ's church next Sunday. Service to commence at 3:30 P. M.

Rev. Mr. Bailey, feeling obliged to give his lungs more rest, has returned to Vineland, N. J., where, free from care and in a more favorable climate, it is earnestly hoped that he may improve and be able soon to return and resume his labors here.
