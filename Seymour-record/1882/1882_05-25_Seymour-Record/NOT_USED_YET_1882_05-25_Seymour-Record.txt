1882_05-25_Seymour-Record

Oxford.

Rev. Mr. Snow holds service next Sunday afternoon at Red Oak at 3:30 o'clock.

The Southford and Oxford nines played a matched game on the green last Saturday. The Southfords came out ahead 26 to 15.

A Seymour nine has, as we understand, challenged the Southfords to meet them here next Saturday at 2 p.m. for a matched game.

Eliza Telgman has been sent to the industrial school at Middletown by the town authorities.

Prof. C. T . Walker gave the organ of St. Peter's church a thorough renovating on Wednesday, to the great discomfiture of the inhabitants thereof.

Tickets for Prof. Russell's reading and recitation entertainment to be given at Souihford on Friday evening, June 2d, are for sale at the post office.

The attendance at the morning service in St. Peters church last Sunday was small and the collection for the library was proportionately so.
