1882_05-04_Seymour-Record.txt

Oxford

Mrs. E. B. Treat is sick with erysipelas.

W. W. Hughes has been building quite an extensive hennery for S. P. Sanford.

Geo. H. Flagg came home last Friday, returning to New York city after a few days visit.

Rev. F. E. Snow and Walter H. Perry are delegates to the Sunday school con-ventioh to be held at Norwich, May 16th, 17th and 18th.

G. W. Flagg took a sudden start last Monday for Goshen, N. Y., via. the N. Y. & N. E. R. R., where he will do some work at a brother's residence.
