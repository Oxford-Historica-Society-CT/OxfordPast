OXFORD REMEMBERS



Jack's Hill Cemetery is an old cemetery.
The cemetery has been occasionally repaired by youth groups in the town.
 


Oxford's Memorial to the Soldiers of World War I.

    In 1931, Oxford erected a bronze tablet, set in stone on the upper green, bearing the names of all those who were in the Armed Services during World War I.
