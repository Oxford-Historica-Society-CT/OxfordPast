-38-
Order on School Society Treasurer for $12.95

7th District, March 20th, 1824.
  Sir please to pay to Oliver Buckingham or bearer twelve dollars and ninety five cents it being the proportion of school money belonging to the Seventh School District in Oxford.
School Society
Abel Wheeler, Esq. treasurer
Oxford, Oct. 20, 1823
David Candee
Cyrus Humphrey , Committee.
* * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * *

    The record also served as a receipt, with Joseph Tucker signing a statement that he received of Abel Wheeler,
"Twelve dollars and ninety five cents on the within order it being in full."

* * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * *

    The funds listed above appear to have been those obtained by the state from the sale of the western reserve lands. Under the system then in use, each town received a portion of these funds. In Oxford these were apparently divided among the school districts on a per pupil basis.

* * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * *
