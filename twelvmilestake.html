Twelve  Mile  Stake
1.  WEBSITE - Colonial Connecticut Records, 1636 - 1776,  <http://www.colonialct.uconn.edu/>
     13 Oct 1774, "Upon the memorial of Enos Gunn, Ebenezer Scott, Sarah Gunn, Jobamah Gunn, Abel Gunn, David Wooster, John Weed, Noah Candee, Ebenezer Osborn, Thomas Osborn, Nathaniel Gunn, praying for the privilege of a school district, beginning at a stake, called Twelve Mile Stake, in the line between Derby and Waterbury .... "

2.  Joseph Anderson, D. D., The Town and City of Waterbury, Connecticut,  [S24]
     vol. 1, p. 360, "In 1733 ... Nathaniel Gunn of Derby bought of John Andrews 137 acres with a house on it, northwardly of Twelve Mile hill."
