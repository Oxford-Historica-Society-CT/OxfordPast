#21  Edward Bassett Gravestone


    This is the gravestone of Edward Bassett, now located at Riverside Methodist Church. It was moved to that location from Zoar Bridge when the Stevenson Bridge was built.  Bassett was a soldier of the American Revolution. The up-wards pointing hand served as a reminder to those he left behind, that they, too, would someday pass from this countryside.,
